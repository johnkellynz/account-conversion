Roles, access & security

Three roles. Australian data. Everything audit-logged.

L1

Rep

Full read / write across all accounts. Owns their state patch. Sees own private notes only.

L2

Manager

Rep permissions + roll-up reports across patches. Can reassign accounts and bid-support requests.

L3

Admin

Manager permissions + user management · audit log · force-logout · "forget contact" PII wipe · admin transfer.

Security posture

2FA sign-inEmail + password + Authenticator app. Re-verify every 30 days.
Kill switchAdmin can force-logout any user from any device, instantly.
EncryptionTLS in transit · AES at rest (Supabase standard).
ANZ residencySupabase Sydney region · serves Australian & NZ data in-region.
Audit logEvery record change captured · visible to admin.
Privacy Acts"Forget contact" PII wipe · AU + NZ Privacy Act aligned.
Daily backupsSupabase Pro · 30-day restore window.
Row-level securityPostgres RLS · users only see what their role allows.

Designed against three named risks

Competitor sees hit list · contractor reads our notes about them · public PII leak. Each mitigated by a specific control above.

Open data sharing — by design

Account data is fully open across reps (no information silos). Personal notes carry a private flag · visible only to owner + admin.

Open decisions & the ask

Five decisions to confirm before build kicks off.

1

Product taxonomy for KPI reporting

Field rep tracks by sector (HVAC / Water / Infra / Mining). Strategy KPIs are by product line (Grooved / Valves / Factory Solutions / Vortex). The monthly pack may need both.

Capture both · pivot in pack
2

PSA tier — auto or manual?

Strategy ties PSA tier to AUD bands. Should tier auto-derive from share-of-wallet snapshot, or stay a manual sales call?

Manual · SOW visible alongside
3

National Tier 1 co-ownership rules

State-patch reps + nationally co-owned Tier 1s need clear lead vs. co-owner permissions in the UI.

Lead rep edits · co-owners comment
4

Outlook email integration scope

Calendar sync is straightforward. Email sync needs MS Graph permissions from Vic IT — significantly more work.

Calendar in Phase 3 · email deferred
5

SAP / global CRM integration appetite

Wanted but depends entirely on Vic IT willingness and timeline. The longest pole in the tent.

Plan without it · add if IT engages

The ask

Management endorsement of the Field CRM as the system of record for executing — and reporting against — the HVAC Key Account Conversion Strategy. Decisions on the five items above so Phase 1 scope can be locked.

What "yes" unlocks

Phase 1 build begins. Pilot rep operates on real Tier 1 data within two weeks. The first auto-rendered monthly pipeline pack lands in management's inbox within the quarter.
